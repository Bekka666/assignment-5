Welcome!

Come visit the Orleans Indoor Pool - with lots of activities for the whole family. Enjoy the gripping water slide or swing off the tarzan rope into a recreational 30 meter pool. Parents, enjoy quality time with your children and the ability to socialize with other parents or relax and take a break in the Sauna. The Orleans Indoor Pool is completely wheelchair accessible, and affordable to all. The Orleans Indoor Pool offers many aquatic courses and activities for everyone to enjoy. Swimming lessons, water aerobics, lap time or even just a simple outing with the family – you name it, we provide it!

Lane Swim

Quiet time, anyone? This is a time for those who wish to exercise without splashing, jumping or diving. This gives people the chance to swim at their own pace or challenge themselves against the clock with some of our personal trainers.

Public Swim

Available for all ages! This is a chance for you to get out of your house and enjoy some quality time with friends and family.

Family Swim

Families, come one, come all! We offer a chance for your kids to socialize with other family and have loads of fun! Toys and lifejackets included.

Preschool Swim

Toddlers, unite! This is a chance for your to expose your toddlers to the exciting sport of swimming and play with other kids their age!
